About Me

I'm Isabella Sciortino, a graduating senior at Berklee College of Music, majoring in Music Business. I'm passionate about A&R, music publishing, and discovering artists who use their voice to spark change. With experience at Universal Music Publishing Group and Berklee’s BPMI, I’ve built strong connections and a love for storytelling through sound. Just a few weeks after graduating, I will be joining Pulse Music Group as an Executive Assistant.

Campus Involvement

During my time at Berklee, I've worked at the Career Center as a student assistant, supporting career development programming and acting as a liaison for Berklee talent. I also served as a Talent Relations Ambassador for the Berklee Popular Music Institute (BPMI), helping bridge the gap between students and music industry professionals. Through these roles, I've helped artists prepare for major music festivals and connect with agencies, managers, and publishers.
